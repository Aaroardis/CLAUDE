## DESCRIPTION

The Future of Love is coming and we're in the midst of a revolution in modern relationships — and the women of Skirt Club? We're the revolutionaries!

In this powerful and provocative Embody Your True Body session, Aaro Ardis will show us what it is to be at the leading edge of love.

Women are bending and breaking outdated relationship rules, blowing past precedent, and creating a new kind of liberated love. As individuals - and as a diffuse global culture of sexually sovereign women - we're opting for custom-crafted love and bespoke relationships that meet our expansive selves. We need to recognize that this moment is revolutionary!

Those of us re-authoring love are expanding our relationships so they can hold sexual autonomy, self-exploration, and radical honesty. We're showing our partners what we're made of - sacred, grounded, sexual energy. We're proving that these aren't threats to partnership. They are medicine for it.

This workshop (ahem, playshop) helps women celebrate themselves as leaders of the Modern Relationship Revolution and authors of the Future of Love. We'll strategize about how to craft love lives spacious enough and strong enough to hold women's freedom and sexual aliveness — and explore the questions living at the center of this revolution:
What does your pleasure teach you?
Who are you when you stop performing?
How much autonomy can love truly hold?
What would love look like if all women moved with deeply-felt security - and total transparency?
And… What if growth wasn't a threat to relationships - but part of the design?

This isn't just personal evolution. This is cultural revolution. The way we love is shaping the future.

## WHAT TO EXPECT

Get ready for a guided, interactive experience.

We'll explore and challenge beliefs, inquire within our somatic selves through some embodied practice, and develop new vision for our love lives. We'll also imagine a radically free future - enjoying the Future of Love as a blank canvas before us.

You'll leave with evolved desires and new relational values — and language for the expansive conversations that will help you love boldly and intentionally into the future. You'll enjoy a felt sense of sexual sovereignty and authorship in love… and a deeper understanding of the evolution of love and relationships happening right now. This event supports women's collective knowing… that we are leading the charge.
