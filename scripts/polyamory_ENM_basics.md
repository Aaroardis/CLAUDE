## SCRIPT 05 — "What Jealousy Is Actually Telling You"
**Status:** `DRAFT`
**Length:** ~35 seconds | **Hook Type:** Reframe / Insight
**Audience:** Direct to man

If you're exploring open relationships and jealousy hits — don't panic.
Jealousy doesn't mean you love her too much.
It doesn't mean polyamory is wrong for you.

Jealousy is a signal.
Usually pointing to one of three things:

A fear that you're not enough.
A belief that love is scarce — that if she loves someone else, there's less for you.
Or an unmet need you haven't named yet.

When you get *curious* about jealousy instead of reactive…
it stops running your relationships.

That's the work. And I'm here for it with you.

**— SEND-TO-HIM VERSION —**
*Caption suggestion: "This is what I want us to work toward together 💛"*

When jealousy comes up for you — and it might —

And I want you to know — you are.

Let's talk about it instead of letting it run us.
