## SALES PSYCHOLOGY TRIGGERS (for hooks & CTAs)
*Source: Instagram/TikTok content by marketing creator — saved April 2026*

The core insight: easiest sales come from people ALREADY watching. They just need to be activated. Five human psychology triggers that do that:

### 1. The Trust YOU Trigger
Don't just be an expert — show why YOU specifically are worth listening to.
- Show personal transformation, not just credentials
- Example framing: "I used to read into every message, every delay, every 'seen'… here's what I changed that made dating feel calm instead of chaotic."

### 2. The "Who They Want to Be" Trigger
Speak to who they're becoming, not just where they are now.
- Example framing: "This is for the man who doesn't want to show up like every other guy… but still wants a relationship that actually goes somewhere."

### 3. The "Does It Work" Trigger
Be specific. No fluffy testimonials.
- Show the shift. Tell the journey from A → B.
- Example framing: "He went from waiting for her to initiate… to being the one who sets the pace without pressure."

### 4. The Emotional Trigger
People buy based on how something makes them feel — then justify it later.
- Don't sell the deliverable. Sell the feeling after.
- ❌ Instead of: "you'll get 3 calls and a workbook"
- ✅ Say: "You'll know exactly how to handle mixed signals, set boundaries early, and stop wasting time on people who aren't right for you."

### 5. The "What Does This Actually Help Me With" Trigger
People don't care what's included. They care what it gives them.
- Lead with the transformation, not the container.
- ❌ Instead of: "5 workbooks and 3 calls"
- ✅ Say: "You'll know exactly how to handle mixed signals, set boundaries early, and stop wasting time on people who aren't right for you."

## CAPTION HOOKS
*See also: SCRIPT_WRITING_ROADMAP.md for full hook typology*

- Desire-based hooks only — no fear, pain, or negativity-driven openers
- Authentic aspiration over manufactured urgency
- Hook should activate curiosity about who they're becoming, not shame about where they are
