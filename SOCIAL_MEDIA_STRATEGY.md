## Platform Strategy

- **TikTok** — better for reach and raw numbers; prioritize going viral and volume

## Content & Production

- Use **AI text on CapCut** — can potentially upload custom font; saves time; more advanced than IG Edits; suppression concern is a myth
- **Skew to volume** — increase output without increasing Nova's fee by Aaro learning CapCut and/or AI tools
- **Loopables** — short, looping content to pull in viewers who prefer quick hits
- **Trending audio** helps reach — avoid boosting with royalty-free music (hurts profile)
- **Double speed watch option** — now available; worth being aware of for pacing
- **Pep talks, meditations, affirmations** — solid content formats to explore
- Play the platform game, but keep content **true to Aaro's voice and brand**

## Trial Reels — What Actually Works (via creator research, April 2026)

Source: Instagram creator with 400+ trial reels posted; firsthand data.

### Two things that make Trial Reels work:

**1. YOUR MESSAGING**
- Most important factor. Find the part of your message that is most understandable to your audience — the line that feels like you're in their head, that shows you deeply understand their desire.
- When your messaging hits, viewers stop scrolling because they know the content is *for them*.
- Reinterpret any hook through the lens of *your* specific audience's situation.

**2. THE LOGISTICS**
- **Keep reels short** — shorter = higher watch time = algorithm keeps pushing it out
- **Long, valuable captions** — not filler; actual substance
- **Fonts: literature or classic only** — no fugly fonts
- **Trending audio works** — doesn't have to be early trending; just trending
- **Post minimum 5 trial reels per day**
- They don't work in the first two weeks — stick with it; they kick in

### Key mindset:
Trial reels function like free ads. Volume + consistency is the game. Once they click, they compound.
